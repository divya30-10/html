Doraemon

Doraemon is the most popular cartoon in the world.Doraemon is a robotic cat from the 22nd century, sent back in time to help Nobita, a clumsy and often bullied boy.Doraemon is known for his kindness,helpfulness, and love for dorayaki (a sweet pancake). It is an entertaining and funny cartoon. He possesses a fourth-dimensional pocket on his stomach from which he pulls out various gadgets to help Nobita. Doraemon films tell adventure stories, such as Nobita meeting a boy from another planet and fighting enemy miners. Doraemon often emphasizes the value of education and knowledge.

Moral:The main moral of the Doraemon cartoon revolves around the importance of friendship, perseverance, and self-improvement.
